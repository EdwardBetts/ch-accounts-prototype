Abridged accounts on the new Companies House service

To file abridged accounts, your accounts must meet at least two of the following:

  • Turnover must not be more than £10.2 million
  • Balance sheet total must not be more than £5.1 million
  • The average number of employees during the year must not be more than 50

You can file abridged accounts on the new Companies House service.

You'll need to login (or register) to file on the new service - your existing WebFiling login details will not work.
