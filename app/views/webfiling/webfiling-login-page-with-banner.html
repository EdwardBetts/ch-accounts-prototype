Private BETA announcement for Abridged Accounts filing service

As a user of our WebFiling service you may be aware of changes in legislation that means you can no longer file abbreviated accounts for accounts covering the 1st of January 2016, onwards.

You may also be aware that there is a new type of accounts that are quite similar, abridged accounts, that are coming into legislation in the near future.

More information

You can file abridged accounts via our online service if:

  • your accounting period starts on or after 1 January 2016
  • your balance sheet doesn’t include 'intangible assets', 'fixed asset investments' or 'revaluation reserve'
  • you just need to supply an 'accounting policies' note (and, if needed, a 'tangible assets' note and/or a 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year’ note)
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to

How to register, use and get help with the service

If you’d like to use the private BETA service, send an email to privatebeta@companieshouse.gov.uk and we’ll get back in touch to advise you of the next steps. This is also the email you can make further correspondence to should you require help with our service.
