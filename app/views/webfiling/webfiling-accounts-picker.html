Abbreviated Accounts

The Companies Act 2006 no longer permits companies to file abbreviated accounts.

Your company will need to file accounts of a different type (full accounts, micro-entity accounts or abridged accounts).

Micro-entity accounts These audit exempt accounts are suitable for companies that fall within the micro-entity definition. New Abridged accounts These audit exempt accounts are suitable for companies to input figures from already prepared and approved abridged accounts. Full accounts with Companies House and HMRC These joint accounts are suitable for small audit exempt full accounts to both Companies House and HMRC. Dormant company accounts These accounts are suitable for companies limited by shares or by guarantee that have never traded.
