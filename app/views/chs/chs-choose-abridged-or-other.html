File abridged accounts

Currently, you can only file abridged accounts using this service.

You can file other types of accounts (dormant, micro-entity, full) using our WebFiling service.

If you’re not sure which types of accounts you want to file, you may want to ask an accountant.

You can file abridged accounts if:

  • your balance sheet doesn’t include 'intangible assets', 'fixed asset investments' or 'revaluation reserve'
  • you only need to supply an 'accounting policies' note (and, if needed, a 'tangible assets' note and/or a 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year’ note)
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to
  • your company meets at least 2 of the following:
    • turnover is no more than £10.2 million
    • balance sheet total is no more than £5.1 million
    • average number of employees is no more than 50
