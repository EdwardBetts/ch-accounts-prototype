File abridged accounts

You can only file abridged accounts here.

If you want to file other types of accounts (micro-entity, abbrevaited, full, or dormant), use our WebFiling service.

If you’re not sure which types of accounts you want to file, you may want to ask an accountant.

Criteria

You can only file abridged accounts if:

  • your balance sheet doesn’t include 'intangible assets', 'fixed asset investments' or 'revaluation reserve'
  • you just need to supply an 'accounting policies' note (and, if needed, a 'tangible assets' note and/or a 'creditors - amounts falling due within one year’ note)
  • you don't need to change the date the accounts are made up to

Your company must also meet at least 2 of the following:

  • turnover is not over £10.2 million
  • balance sheet total is not over £5.1 million
  • average number of employees is not over 50
