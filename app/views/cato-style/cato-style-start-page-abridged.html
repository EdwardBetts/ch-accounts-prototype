Abridged accounts

Abridged accounts must meet at least 2 of the following criteria:

  1. Turnover can't exceed £10.2 millionIf you sell books and the total sales of books during the accounting period amounts to £10,000 then that is your turnover.
  2. The balance sheet total can't be more than £5.1 millionThis is the sum of the total fixed assets plus total current assets
  3. There can't be more than an average of 50 employees during the year
