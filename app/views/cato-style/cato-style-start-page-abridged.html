Abridged accounts

Abridged accounts must meet at least 2 of the following criteria:

  1. Turnover can't exceed £10.2 million
  2. The balance sheet total can't be more than £5.1 million (this is the sum of the total fixed assets plus total current assets)
  3. There can't be more than an average of 50 employees during the year

Before you start

  • The abridged accounts need to be prepared, with figures rounded to whole numbers. Round down income, and round up spending.
  • The abridged accounts should have been approved by the director/board of directors.
