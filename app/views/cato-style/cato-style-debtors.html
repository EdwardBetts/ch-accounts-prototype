Debtors
Trade debtors

Trade debtors are customers who owe money for goods or services your company has provided as part of its normal trading activities.

Don’t include any bad or doubtful debts you’re aware of or have made provision for.

Trade debtors who are due to settle after more than one year should be reported as additional information.

Prepayments and accrued income

Prepayments are goods or services your company has paid for, but has not received (as at the end of the accounting period).

Accrued income is income earned by your company (and included in your turnover), but not yet charged for (invoiced) – this type of income is not a trade debtor or other debtor.

Other debtors

Other debtors are customers owing money who are not included as trade debtors.

Other debtors who are due to settle after more than one year should be reported as additional information.

Total

The total of all debtors for the accounting period.

This is calculated by adding together trade debtors, prepayments and accrued income, and other debtors.
