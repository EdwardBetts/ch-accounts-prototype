Fixed assets

Intangible assets

The value of the company's non-physical assets - for example, patents, trademarks, licences, goodwill (taking account of depreciation).

Tangible assets

The value of the company's physical assets used in the business - for example, machinery, fixtures and fittings, office equipment, vehicles, buildings, land (taking account of depreciation).

Total fixed assets

'Intangible assets' plus 'tangible assets'.

Current assets

Stocks

The value of goods or services bought or made, but not yet sold - for example, raw materials, balance of long-term contracts.

Debtors

Amount owed to the company - for example, debts, payments or income owed but not yet received.

Cash at bank and in hand

Total cash available on demand, either in the bank or in petty cash.

Total current assets

'Stocks' plus 'debtors' plus 'cash at bank and in hand'.

Creditors - amounts falling due within one year

Debts you expect to settle within a year - for example, unpaid supplier invoices, bank loans or overdrafts.

Net current assets (liabilities)

Total assets less current liabilities

'Total fixed assets' plus 'net current assets or liabilities'.

Creditors - amounts falling due after more than one year

Debts you expect to settle after more than 1 year - for example, bank loans with instalments due after more than a year.

Provision for liabilities

An amount set aside to cover future costs - for example, for tax, costs of restructuring or providing pensions.

Accruals and deferred income

Total net assets (liabilities)

'Total assets less current liabilities' minus 'creditors amounts falling due after more than 1 year' minus 'provision for liabilities'. This must match 'total shareholders' funds'.

Capital and reserves

Revaluation reserve

How much assets have changed in value due to being revalued, compared to their previous values on the balance sheet - for example, to reflect market value.

Profit and loss account

Total cash available on demand, either in the bank or in petty cash.

Total shareholders' funds

'Called up share capital' plus 'revaluation reserve' plus 'profit and loss account'. This must match 'total net assets or liabilities'.
