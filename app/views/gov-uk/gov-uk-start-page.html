File your confirmation statement (annual return) or accounts with Companies House

The confirmation statement has replaced the annual return to Companies House.

Use this service to:

  • file your confirmation statement (previously annual return)
  • file your company accounts - you might be able to file this jointly with your tax return for HM Revenue and Customs (HMRC)
  • sign up for email alerts to let you know when your confirmation statement and accounts are due

If you were due to file an annual return by 30 June 2016, use this service to file your annual return instead of a confirmation statement.
