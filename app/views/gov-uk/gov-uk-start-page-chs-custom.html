File company annual accounts with Companies House

Use this service to file your company annual accounts with Companies House

Limited liability partnerships can’t use this service - send your annual accounts directly to Companies House.

If you've a private limited company that doesn’t need an auditor, you could file your company accounts through the same service as your company tax return.

Before you start

  • Your accounts need to be prepared, with figures rounded to whole numbers. Round down income, and round up spending.
  • Your accounts should have been approved by the director/board of directors.
  • Read guidance on what your annual accounts should include.

Get help

Ask Companies House for help if you:

  • don’t have a computer or internet access
  • want to use the online service but need some help
